What Is This Site For?

An eponymous term is one which is derived from somebody's name. Famous examples used in surgical practice include Graves disease, McBurney's point, & Strasberg's critical view of safety. When used correctly, eponymous terms can enhance communication, shared understanding & can help with decision-making. These features will help you quickly deepen your understanding

Until now, to better understand these useful terms, we have been limited dictionaries and encylopedias that index items alphabetically. But because of the rich meaning and historical background of each term, we imagined a better resource to catalogue & learn these fascinating terms.
